About

Step inside an eclectic atmosphere not found anywhere else. We have 18 beers on tap (including some amazing craft beers) and a large selection of wines. Kick back and enjoy great food and watch the game on our numerous TV's.

We offer games you can play solo or with friends with the largest being our 12 ft. shuffleboard table.

We can accommodate large groups and teams. Ask about our team sponsorship program.
